How to use the Toto Wala



1. Create an account


All you need is an email address and phone number. You can request a ride from your
browser or from the Toto Wala. To download the app, go to the App Store or Google Play Store

2. Enter your destination


Open the app and enter where you’re going in the Where to? box. Tap to confirm your
pickup location and tap Confirm again to be matched to a driver nearby.

3.Meet your Toto wala


You can track their arrival on the map. When they’re a few minutes away, wait for them at your pickup location.

4.Check your ride


Every time you take a trip with Toto Wala, please make sure you’re getting into the right toto
with the right driver by matching the license plate,
Toto make and model, and driver photo with what’s provided in your app.
Toto Wala trips can only be requested through the app, so never get in a Toto where the vehicle or driver identity doesn't match what’s displayed in your app.

5.Sit back and relax


When you arrive, payment is easy. Depending on your region, you have options. Use cash
or a payment method like a credit card or Uber Cash balance.

6.Pay online & rate your ride


Let us know how your trip went. You can also give your driver a compliment or add a tip
in the app.
